Abilities, Uses and importance of R2D2


R2 communicates in an audible binary language

makes the droid capable of reading, communicating and

manipulating complex computer systems to serve whoever

has ownership at the moment. The droids abilities to

repair and maintain vessels makes this him very useful

during engagements and in peacetime. Having these skills

allows him the luxery of always being useful and frequently

putting him in the right place at the right time. Most key

events in the story can be somehow tied to his involvement.
